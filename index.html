2. Business Overview

Kukuzetu is a retail and distribution business supplying everyday protein and food products to households across Nairobi.

These are high-frequency food purchases with predictable demand, generating consistent daily cashflow.

3. 2025 Operational Reset

Rapid expansion in early 2025 exposed operational weaknesses:
• aggressive price reductions
• increased distribution volume
• stock control weaknesses

In May 2025, management implemented a full operational reset:
• strict daily reconciliation
• leadership restructuring
• branch-level profit tracking
• accountability systems

Results:
• reduced stock leakage
• improved margin control
• restored profitability
Kukuzetu now scales from operational discipline rather than experimentation.

6. Growth Strategy

Kukuzetu will grow through three complementary channels.

1 — Wholesale Expansion

Supply:

• supermarkets

• restaurants

• small retailers

• food vendors

Service Locker Rooms

Wholesale increases inventory turnover and improves supplier pricing leverage.
2 — Strategic Branch Network

10 retail branches

Branch expansion focuses on high-density residential areas with strong food demand.

Distribution capability also supports the wholesale expansion strategy.

8. Investment Options:


Kukuzetu is raising KES 10,000,000. Investors may choose between two structures:


Option A — Revenue Participation

Investment: KES 10,000,000

Structure:

• investor receives 5% of monthly revenue

• payments scale with business performance

• repayment capped at 1.8× investment

Example scenario:

Target monthly revenue: KES 5M

Investor share: KES 250,000/month

Total repayment cap: KES 18,000,000

Estimated repayment period: ~6 years

Option B — Fixed Interest Loan

Investment: KES 10,000,000

Structure:

• approximately 18% annual interest (reducing balance)

• 6-year amortization

Monthly payment: ~KES 250,000

Total repayment: ~KES 18,000,000

9. Why Kukuzetu Wins

Kukuzetu operates in one of the most resilient sectors of the Kenyan economy:everyday protein consumption.
